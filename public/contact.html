FAQ

Do I need an account?

Nope. No sign-ups, no logins. Just browse and enjoy the funny GIFs instantly.

Can I upload an image?

We don't host uploads. Share a link to your image, and we'll help you find or create something amazing.

What formats do you support?

We work with standard web formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP. If it loads in a browser, we can handle it.

Can you blur or crop?

Basic edits like cropping and blurring are possible. Just describe what you need, and we'll make it happen.

Can you remove watermarks?

We respect creators' rights. We won't remove watermarks or bypass copyright protections.

How do I credit sources?

Always link back to the original creator when possible. Respect the work, share the love.
